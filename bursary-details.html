Bursary Description

Company offers bursaries to support students pursuing studies in STEM fields (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics). The bursary covers tuition fees, accommodation, and a stipend for living expenses. Applicants must be South African citizens, demonstrate financial need, and maintain a minimum academic average of 70%. The application deadline for the upcoming academic year is 25 May 2024

Benefits: Full Tuition Fee, Accomodation, Books, Meal Allowance

Requirements
  • Undergraduate or Post Graduate qualification within STEM.
  • 70% Matric Results with Maths
  • 65% Recent Academic Results
